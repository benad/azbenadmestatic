So, my blog, blog.benad.me is finally on Squarespace. To summarize the setup:

  • blog.benad.me hosted by Squarespace.
  • RSS feed proxied and cached by Google Feedburner.
  • Twitter posts on my Twitter account automatically posted from my RSS feed by twitterfeed.com.
  • Short URLs using "s.benad.me" by bit.ly.

I still have to finish customizing the (fully customizable) CSS of my blog to somewhat match my main site, but I'm not in the rush for that.

A few immediate reactions about the new setup:

  • Squarespace is fast. And I mean fast. I've tried LiveJournal, Tumblr, Posterous and Wordpress, and all tasks like loading pages and using the search box are several times faster on Squarespace. I guess you get what you pay for.
  • Squarespace's main and only business is their site and blog hosting using their own software, and is profitable. Not a "for sale" startup or a part of a bigger conglomerate that will gladly sell your data.
  • Professional at last. Everything's using my domain benad.me, and they're all on professional, real, reliable wb hosting (Squarespace and Fused).

On my TODO list:

  • Any way to post with markdown email on Squarespace? Email posting works, but plaintext only.

So, here goes nothing.
