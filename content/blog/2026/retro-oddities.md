### Sprawl on HyperCard

At this point, I've read and owned William Gibson's [Sprawl trilogy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sprawl_trilogy) in multiple form factors, but none seem more time-appropriate than its release as [HyperCard stacks for Macintosh](https://macintoshgarden.org/apps/neuromancer-count-zero-mona-lisa-overdrive). It may be the least convenient way to read those books though.

It works well on [InfiniteMac](https://infinitemac.org/)'s [System 7.5 virtual machine](https://infinitemac.org/1994/System%207.5): You can load it in the VM using the "Macintosh Garden" tab at the bottom, and the compressed archive will show up in the path "The Outside World:Downloads".

### Stereoscopic Doom on the Nintendo 3DS

Sure, DOOM has been ported to countless platforms, but its port to the Nintendo 3DS offers a unique experience unavailable elsewhere: Playing DOOM in glass-free stereoscopic 3D.

Hacking the 3DS is not too difficult if you [follow this guide](https://3ds.hacks.guide/), and then you can install [PrBoom-Plus-3DS](https://github.com/Voxel9/PrBoom-Plus-3DS). Performance is reasonable on the 3DS that I own, though I've heard it is better on the "new 3DS".
