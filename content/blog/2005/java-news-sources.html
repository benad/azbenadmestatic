---toml title = "Java News Sources" date = "2005-09-07T17:23:00-04:00" permalink = "blog/2005/9/7/java-news-sources/" --- How do I manage to know what's the latest buzz in Java development?

First, I subscribe to the RSS feed at http://del.icio.us/tag/java . There's a lot of noise, but I myself post my bookmarks on del.icio.us, especially interesting Java ones, so there's a lot of good things there.

I also look at http://www.java-source.net/last-projects . There's no RSS feed there, but it's quite good and worth frequent visits.

Finally, I listen to podcast JavaCast. It's usually way too long (it's now 2 hours per episode!), but is absolutely great.

So, in summary:

  • http://del.icio.us/tag/java
  • http://www.java-source.net/last-projects
  • JavaCast
