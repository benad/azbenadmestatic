I’ve recently seen the trailer of Game of Thrones Season 2 on YouTube, which should roughly correspond to the book “A Clash of King”, book two of the “A Song of Ice and Fire” series by George R. R. Martin. The first season was an incredibly well made adaptation of the book, and hopefully the second season will be just as good.

I still haven’t finished reading the second book, so I was relieved that the trailer didn’t spoil too much the story. I just hope I’ll have the time to finish reading it by the time the season starts on April first, but I really tend to procrastinate too much when reading long books. I suspect I’ll have to read it on my iPhone rather than listening to audio books to place me in the mood of finishing it soon, as my list of books I was to read is now becoming long.
