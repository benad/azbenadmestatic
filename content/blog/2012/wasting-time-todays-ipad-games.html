Since I'm on vacation, I wasted a few hours playing some games on my iPad. Here are the ones I recommend.

The Room, not to be confused with the movie of the same name, is a nice puzzle game where you try to unlock some contraption device. A bit short, but looks amazing and plays great.

Blowup!! is an action puzzler where you simply try to eliminate large enough group of colours. You've surely played on of those flash games where you have a grid of coloured bubbles, and clicking on a group of bubbles of the same colour eliminates them. Well, it's that, but time-based, with a few twists, and amazing music that progressively changes as you get further. It's just an excuse for me to play until I can unlock all that great music.

Interlocked, a nice sliding blocks puzzle, but this time in 3D. This can be quite challenging.

Finally, Wonderputt, a nice, single-screen mini-putt game. The graphics look amazing, and the physics are just perfect, making it just as challenging as a real-life mini-putt, but this time with aliens, giant frogs, ski slopes and a rocket spaceship.
