A quick post while it’s still relevant. Tonight I’m following the United States presidential election coverage using my iPad. Here’s what I’m using:

  • ABC News for iPad. 3 live video streams that work in Canada? Nice… Still, I prefer multitasking in the web browser with my iPad 4th generation.
  • Radio Canada (French), taking the live results page feed from AP, and even offering live TV viewing of RDI or the radio within the same page.
  • CNN’s live result page. Their iPad app is not so good, so I just copy-pasted the link of the live result page embedded in their app’s browser into Safari instead.
  • Huffington Post’s result page.
  • A nice interactive summary of possible outcomes by the New York Times.
  • Of course, Twitter.
