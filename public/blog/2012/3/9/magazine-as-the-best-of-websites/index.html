The free magazine The Redditor is a great example of magazines built up from the contents of a web site, in this case Reddit. Like Distro for Engadget, but most importantly Macworld, PC Mag and many others, magazines ends up still existing in digital form, with great layout and typography, and at the same time still serves a purpose to summarize the deluge of daily contents from a web site, plus a few long articles as exclusives.

So, magazines are not dead, but need web sites to build on. That works for me.
