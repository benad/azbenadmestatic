A few days ago I was trying to see if there was a decent book about Debian 6 on Safari Books through our corporate account at work, and found absolutely nothing. After seeing this post on Slashdot, I discovered the Debian Administrator's Handbook. While I wouldn't have minded the French version (I speak French), the English one is free, and seems pretty complete. So I went ahead with a small donation and transfered the MOBI file to my Kindle.

I'll report back in a few week with a review of the book, of course. I already have some experience with Debian 5, so maybe I won't learn much, but considering I may have to set up a new Debian machine at work pretty soon I'll be able to make a review in the context of how useful it was.
